Our Services

We are constantly adding new services to our list, so check back periodically to see if we have anything new!

Speakers and Seminars

We can record or live stream speakers in your event using a simple camera setup or we can incorporate more cameras for whole-room coverage. Additionally, we can include a clean view of the presenter's screen in the video.

Interviews and Talking Heads

Need simple interview shots or walk-and-talk videos to help boost your brand? We can shoot multi-angle interviews and incorporate a teleprompter for straight-on speaking into the camera to boost your audience's engagement.

Aerial Photography and Video

We can record aerial video for a specific production need or just for you to have on file for promotional use. Additionally, we can record aerial video in tight spaces or even indoors in large open areas. Our pilot is a licensed UAV pilot under the FAA Part 107 guidlines.

Multi-platform

We can stream in any resolution desired, from 4K for large screens to 720p for mobile live streams.

Archive Digitization

We can convert your old media formats into digital files for cloud storage and backups. Currently we can digitize VHS, VHS-C, 8mm tape, MiniDV, audio cassettes, or old DVDs.

Robust Content Delivery & plugins

We will provide secure download links of the completed content from our own self-hosted servers and keep backups for up to 30 days after final video delivery. Also, any live streams will be locally recorded in the event of an issue with the venue infrastructure.

On-Location Productions

If you need a video shot on-location like a moving vehicle, large outdoor structure, extreme conditions like underwater or even conditions with hazardous materials or weather, we can design a rig for your needs and capture it with multiple cameras in the best way possible.

Consultation

We provide consultation services for small venues looking to set up live streaming systems.
